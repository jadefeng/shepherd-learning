# Fall Safety: Don’t Rely on Luck

## Transcript

Last year, more than **1,000 workers** were seriously injured in falls from elevation.

Just because you haven’t fallen yet doesn’t make you safe.  
It just makes you lucky.

Stop relying on luck to keep you safe.

If you’re using a ladder for access:
- Tie the ladder off  
- Ensure it has a secure base  
- Make sure the top extends at least **one metre (three feet)** above the roof surface  

For more information, visit **worksafebc.com**.
